Welcome.

This reader helps you move through text faster by showing one word at a time.

A good way to use it:
First ask AI to summarize a long paper or article.
Then paste the summary here and read it with focus.

How it works.

Your eyes stay fixed.
Words appear in the same position.
The red letter marks the visual center, helping recognition and reducing eye movement.

How to begin.

Start slow.
Choose a comfortable speed.
Increase gradually as reading feels smooth and clear.

Tips.

Relax your eyes.
Do not chase the words.
Use arrow keys to adjust speed or step through text.
Press space to play or pause.

Enjoy.
